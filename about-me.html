Information about the designer

Even though I'm currently a college player for Bushnell University, my soccer journey started way before this. The love for the soccer ball began when I was just 2 years old. From playing on the futsal court of my neighborhood to watching soccer games all day with my dad, soccer has always been my passion. In my journey to this point, I had the privilege of playing for various clubs and competing against strong opponents. I’ve played for clubs like Sao Paulo, Palmeiras, Santos, Florida Elite, and Dumiense, and I’ve won the Sao Paulo State Championship and three ECNL Southeast Conference titles. I hope this new chapter in my life with my new team brings many friendships and happy experiences.
